Ottawa ICA

The following set of documents is based on the presentation of Rob Weibel, on behalf of the project AGENT, at the 19th International Cartographic Conference in Ottawa, on the 20th of August 1999. An acknowledgements slide has been added and those illustrations that clearly represent personal contributions by individual researchers in the AGENT consortium have been labelled.

The documents are the paper and the set of slides in two formats.

The paper

The paper is in pdf format. The size of this file is more than 400 kb.

The text of the slides

The text gives a quick overview of the slides, but the missing of images makes them difficult to read.
