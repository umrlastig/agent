The partners of AGENT

The partners of AGENT are :

  • Institut Géographique National (France),
  • Laser-Scan Ltd (UK),
  • Institut National Polytechnique de Grenoble (France),
  • University of Edinburgh (UK),
  • University of Zurich (Switzerland).

The team of AGENT is composed of a mix of researchers, developers and users and the project builds on past collaborations and visits among the partners as well as:

  • experience in research and production of a national mapping agency,
  • the know-how of a developer of an advanced Geographical Information System (GIS) that utilizes active object oriented database technology,
  • and the expertise in cartography, topological modelling and multi-agent techniques of three renown research teams.
