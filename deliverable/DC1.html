Title of Deliverable

Selection of Basic Measures

 

Abstract
This report provides a state of the art for measures for cartographic generalisation. It offers a classification and points out measures commonly in use, as well as new approaches. After an assessment, a justification of the selection of measures for the project prototype is presented.
