Project AGENT

AGENT was a research and development project funded by the ESPRIT programme (ESPRIT 24939). It started on the 1st of December 1997 and it has been closed since the 5th of December 2000.

The following documents give a brief overview of the project :

  • Objectives
  • Partners

The public deliverables are:

  • Generalisation Modelling using agent paradigm
  • Constraint Analysis
  • Selection of Basic Measures
  • Specifications for measures on Meso level and organisation
  • Selection of Basic Algorithms
  • Strategic Algorithms Using Organisations
  • Technology Implementation Plan

The available papers and slides are:

  • paper and the slides presented at the 19th International Cartographic Conference in Ottawa, on the 20th of August 1999.
  • paper presented at 5th EC-GIS Workshop, Stresa, Italy, on June 1999.
